How the integration of Artificial Intelligence And Blockchain is revolutionizing the IT industry.


Artificial Intelligence and blockchain are hot and emerging technologies for the past few years. AI has evolved into leading technologies and powering innovation in almost every industry. While Blockchain plays an amazing role in securing our digital data, assuring privacy, and eliminating frauds. Though there are numerous benefits of both AI and Blockchain technology, there are also some drawbacks of both for example the artificial brain of an AI is fed with a large amount of data which is usually collected from users like us, this has raised a long debatable discussion on user's data privacy. On the other hand, blockchain is questioned on the factor of scalability, Data management issues, and it's high energy dependency.

But with combination of these two mighty technologies they help each other to overcome their flaws, unlock their maximum potential for our benefit, and create a totally new paradigm.
Here are some key benefits list down below.


Data Protection

AI at root requires a huge amount of data, which raises challenge of storing data securely. The whole point of blockchain is to decentralize the storage of data this allows the creation of a highly secure database system that can be accessed by only permitted parties and are almost unhackable by breachers. When merging AI with blockchain, we get a secure system for sensitive valuable personal data of individuals. Such Systems would always improve them by learning and become more secure. AI could access the data only with permission, but once it gets through the proper procedure it can provide us with the best results.


Explicitness in process

As AI is becoming smarter it gets more complex and it is becoming difficult to understand what goes on under the hood and how does it conclude a result. But with the addition of blockchain, data scientists could understand every step, they can access all the immutable records of the procedure. This will make the auditing process easier. Whenever an AI result goes wrong, an inspection of the root cause can be easily done through these records. This makes the process transparent and explicit.


Data Monetization

Monetization of data by tech giants like Facebook and Google is a huge revenue source for them and is not new. This data is collected by their users and sold to a third party which has lifted the issue of data privacy. But with blockchain allowing cryptographical storage of data, we could monetize our data personally without compromising sensitive information. For AI models, companies could directly buy data from such data marketplaces. This will reduce the exploitation of user's data by tech giants.


Breakthrough for small companies

A lower market in IT could bypass the monopoly ownership of the tech giant on data. With a Decentralize Data marketplace where individuals sell data on a large scale, these companies can access otherwise too expensive, privately kept data and use it for their personalized AI. The spread of AI within small companies would provide enormous progress in the industry and open doors to new job opportunities.


Production and Deployment on blockchain hosting

When it comes to the deployment of the product cloud service are not as secure as blockchain. Blockchain regulated with an AI can be used for a decentralized hosting of applications, Providing with best security measures that are possible. AI improves the operational efficiency of such services.
